Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

StackBid ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use stackbid.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email address — when you request a PDF estimate or sign up for price alerts
  • Name and role — when you unlock a full estimate
  • ZIP code — to provide local pricing and contractor matches
  • Project description — to generate your materials estimate
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your materials estimate
  • To send you your estimate PDF by email (if requested)
  • To send weekly price alerts for your area (if opted in)
  • To improve our pricing accuracy and product features
  • To connect you with local contractors and suppliers (with your consent)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Contractors — only if you explicitly request a quote from a specific contractor
  • Service providers — such as email delivery services (Resend) and database services (Supabase), under strict data protection agreements
  • Legal requirements — if required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@stackbid.app.

5. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for functionality only (e.g., remembering your estimate preferences). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure database access controls, and rate limiting to protect your information.

8. Children's Privacy

StackBid is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at:
